What We Do

Our Services

Tick control, mosquito spraying, tree care, termite protection, and general exterminating. One call covers it all.

01

Tick Control

Barrier spray treatments applied every 4 to 6 weeks through the season. We target the wooded edges, lawn perimeters, and brushy areas where ticks live and wait. Safe for kids and pets once dry. We always call before arriving and leave a detailed note after every treatment.

02

Mosquito Spraying

Take back your backyard. Our mosquito treatments target breeding zones, standing water areas, and shaded resting spots so your family can enjoy summer evenings, cookouts, and outdoor time without the constant bite. Applied on a seasonal schedule throughout Suffolk County.

03

Tree Spraying

Protect your trees and shrubs from damaging insects, fungal disease, and parasites before the damage becomes irreversible. We treat a wide range of common Long Island tree pests and report what we find at every visit so you always know the health of your property.

04

Termite Protection

Comprehensive termite inspection and bait station monitoring with regular follow-up visits. We assess the problem, apply the right treatment, and check back to make sure it is working. You receive a written report after every visit so nothing is left to guesswork.

05

General Exterminating

Bees, wasps, yellow jackets, ants, beetles, rodents, and more. If it is unwanted on your property, we handle it with safe, targeted treatments that address the source, not just the symptom. We are thorough, we are careful, and we follow up.

06

Seasonal Programs

Year-round protection plans covering tick and mosquito season from first thaw to late fall. One call and you are on our schedule for the season. We handle the timing, the reminders, and the follow-ups so you do not have to think about it.

Who We Are

The Business Behind
the Business.

All Purpose Landscaping, Inc. has been based in Middle Island, New York since 1978. We are a family-owned, family-run operation with deep roots in the Suffolk County communities we serve.

We are not a franchise and we are not a national chain. There is no 1-800 number. When you call us, a real person answers. When we show up, we work with the same attention to detail we would give our own property.

Over nearly five decades, we have built a reputation one yard at a time. Customers who first called us in the 1980s still call today, and their children call us now too. That kind of loyalty is not something you can manufacture. It is earned every single visit.

We notify you before every visit. We leave a written note when you are not home. We are careful around your kids, your pets, and your plantings. We report what we find so you always know the condition of your property. And if something is not right, we make it right.

That is all we have ever done. That is all we plan to keep doing.
